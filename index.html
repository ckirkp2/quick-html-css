We maintain an up-to-date and real-time analysis of fight odds, letting you know who is the underdog and favorite going into each fight.

Need some help with the odds?

This site utilizes the American odds format, which is the standard in combat sports. E.G. a fighter with a (+235) underdog bet using $100 on the line will net the winner $235. On the other hand, if an opposing fighter is a (-275) favorite, then $275 must be risked to win $100.
